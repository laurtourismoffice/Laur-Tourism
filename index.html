About Laur

The Heart of Nueva Ecija

Laur is a first-class municipality in Nueva Ecija, Philippines — known for its rich natural landscapes, warm community, and deep cultural heritage at the foot of the Sierra Madre mountain range.

Whether you seek adventure in nature, a glimpse of local history, or simply a peaceful escape — Laur has something for every traveler.

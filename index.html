About Laur

The Heart of Nueva Ecija

Laur is a first-class municipality in Nueva Ecija, Philippines — known for its rich natural landscapes, warm communities, and deep cultural heritage nestled at the foothills of the Sierra Madre mountain range.

Whether you are seeking adventure in nature, a glimpse into local history, or simply a peaceful retreat — Laur has something for every traveler.

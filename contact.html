How to Get There

Getting to Laur

Laur is easily accessible from Manila and nearby cities in Nueva Ecija.

From Manila

Take a bus from Cubao or Araneta to Cabanatuan City, then ride a jeepney or tricycle to Laur.

About 3–4 hours
From Cabanatuan City

Drive via General Tinio Street heading east toward Laur. Follow road signs for Laur, Nueva Ecija.

About 1–1.5 hours
From Nearby Towns

Tricycles and jeepneys are available from Gabaldon, Bongabon, and nearby municipalities.

About 20–40 minutes
